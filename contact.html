Information to mention initially

  • Your city and residential status
  • Main income types: salary, house property, capital gains, business/profession or other sources
  • Whether F&O, intraday, foreign income/assets or NRI facts are involved
  • Whether AIS and Form 26AS broadly match your records
  • Whether the enquiry is for a fresh, revised, belated or updated return
Do not send initially: income-tax portal passwords, OTPs, bank passwords or unprotected public links containing PAN/Aadhaar documents.

Professional acceptance
