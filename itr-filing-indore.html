What is usually reviewed?

  • Salary/Form 16 and bank-interest reporting
  • Capital gains and investment transactions
  • F&O, intraday and trading losses
  • Business or professional income
  • AIS, Form 26AS and TDS reconciliation
  • Residential status and NRI facts where relevant

Local and online ITR workflow

Taxpayers in Indore can begin with a factual online enquiry. The first review identifies the income heads, likely return-form issues and documents still required. Documents should be shared only through the confirmed professional process; never send an income-tax portal password or OTP through a public comment, unknown link or social-media post.

The same document-review workflow can be used for an online engagement elsewhere in India, subject to case facts, document availability and professional acceptance.

Before filing

Form 16 alone may not contain bank interest, investments, capital gains, rent, foreign assets or other reportable information. AIS and Form 26AS should be reconciled with source documents, and the old/new regime comparison should use the complete income profile.

Professional note: Exact ITR form, tax treatment and filing approach depend on documents and facts. No refund, tax saving or notice-free outcome is guaranteed.
