A salary return is not always only Form 16

Form 16 is a starting document. A complete return review may also require savings-account and deposit interest, house-property details, investment transactions, prior-employer salary, eligible deductions, tax payments and information appearing in AIS or Form 26AS.

Common salary-return checks

  • Both Form 16s where employment changed during the year
  • AIS and Form 26AS reconciliation
  • Bank interest and other income outside payroll
  • HRA, deductions and supporting records
  • Capital gains or trading activity that can change the ITR form
  • Old/new regime comparison using the complete facts

When the form may change

ITR-1 is not a universal form for every salaried person. Capital gains, foreign assets, directorship, unlisted equity shares, certain losses and other disqualifying facts may require another return form. Use the AY 2026-27 form-selection guide before relying on salary status alone.
