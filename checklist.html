General ITR document checklist

This checklist is for initial preparation only. Final documents depend on your facts, income type and applicable law.

Common documents

  • PAN and basic contact details
  • Form 16, if salaried
  • AIS and Form 26AS
  • Bank account details for refund verification
  • Bank interest and other income details
  • Deductions/investment proofs, if applicable

Salary cases

  • Form 16 from employer
  • Salary slips, if needed
  • Rent/HRA details, if claimed
  • Home loan interest certificate, if applicable
  • Details of job change / multiple Form 16, if applicable

Capital gains

  • Broker capital gain statement
  • Mutual fund capital gain statement
  • Property sale/purchase documents, if applicable
  • Details of expenses/exemptions claimed, if any

F&O / Intraday / Trading

  • Broker P&L report
  • Turnover report, if available
  • Expense details, if claimed
  • Bank statement if required for verification

Business / Profession

  • Sales/receipts and expense details
  • Bank statements
  • GST/TDS details, if applicable
  • Loan, asset and investment details, if relevant

NRI / Foreign income

  • Residency details and travel dates
  • Indian income details
  • Foreign income/asset details, if applicable
  • Tax residency / DTAA documents, if applicable
