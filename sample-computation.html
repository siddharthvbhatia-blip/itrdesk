Review checklist normally accompanying a computation

  • Name, PAN, status and assessment year
  • Income head classification and schedules
  • Old/new regime and deduction eligibility
  • Special-rate income and loss set-off
  • TDS, TCS, advance tax and self-assessment tax
  • Final reconciliation with AIS, Form 26AS and official utility
