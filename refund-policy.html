Refund and cancellation policy.

Effective 16 July 2026.

Digital products

The paid PDF/Word computation and paid ITR preparation JSON are separate digital products generated from customer-entered data immediately after payment verification. Once a purchased product has been successfully generated or downloaded, that product is treated as delivered and its fee is ordinarily non-refundable.

Eligible refund situations

A refund may be considered where the same customer was charged more than once for the same product and exact payload, payment was captured but the purchased product could not be generated because of a verified technical failure, or the product was not delivered within a reasonable period after the customer reported the issue.

Not eligible

A refund is not ordinarily available merely because the customer entered incorrect or incomplete information, changed the computation or draft after payment, expected professional review or ITR filing to be included, expected the preparation draft to be an official upload-ready JSON, no longer requires the product, or disagrees with an indicative output that must be reconciled with records and the official utility.
