Effective 13 July 2026.

Digital computation service

The paid computation is a digital service generated from customer-entered figures immediately after payment verification. Once either the PDF or Word computation has been successfully generated or downloaded, the service is treated as delivered and the fee is ordinarily non-refundable.

Eligible refund situations

A refund may be considered where the same customer was charged more than once for the same computation, payment was captured but neither document could be generated because of a verified technical failure, or the service was not delivered within a reasonable period after the customer reported the issue.

Not eligible

A refund is not ordinarily available merely because the customer entered incorrect or incomplete information, changed the computation after payment, expected ITR filing to be included, no longer requires the report, or disagrees with an indicative result that must be reconciled with records and the official utility.
