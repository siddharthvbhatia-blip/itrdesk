Questions taxpayers ask first

Quick, factual answers.

Which ITR form should I use?

It depends on residential status, income heads, business or professional income, capital gains, foreign assets, total income and other exclusions. Use the finder as a starting point and confirm against the notified form and utility.

Does the free calculator upload my figures?

No. Free calculator figures are processed in your browser. Information is sent only if you deliberately proceed to the separate secure computation flow.

Is Form 16 enough?

Not always. AIS, Form 26AS, bank interest, capital gains, deductions, taxes paid and regime choice should also be checked.

Is a refund guaranteed?

No. It depends on applicable law, complete facts, taxes paid, return processing and any adjustment by the Income Tax Department.

Should I share my income-tax password?

No. Never send portal passwords, OTPs, card PINs or banking passwords. Identity and document steps should be discussed only after you choose to make an enquiry.

Can online enquiries be handled outside Indore?

Yes, subject to identity, documents, scope, applicable law, conflict checks and professional acceptance.
