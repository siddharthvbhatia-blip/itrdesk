Common questions

Only the essentials.

Which ITR form should I use?

The form depends on residential status, income heads, capital gains, business income, foreign assets and other conditions. Use the tools as a starting point and confirm the final form after reviewing all facts.

Is Form 16 enough?

Not always. AIS, Form 26AS, bank interest, capital gains, deductions and taxes paid should also be checked.

Should I share my income-tax password or OTP?

No. Do not share portal passwords, OTPs, banking PINs or card credentials through the website or public messages.
