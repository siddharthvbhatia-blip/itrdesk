Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Is Form 16 enough for filing ITR?

Form 16 is important, but AIS, Form 26AS, bank interest, capital gains, deductions and regime selection should also be checked.

Do I need to share my income-tax portal password?

Please do not share passwords through Instagram DM or public comments. A safe document/process will be discussed after enquiry.

Is refund guaranteed?

No. Refund depends on law, income details, TDS, taxes paid and processing by the department. No refund guarantee is made.

Can investors file simple ITR-1?

Cases involving capital gains usually need careful ITR form selection. Final form depends on facts and documents.

What if AIS and Form 16 do not match?

Mismatch should be reviewed before filing because it may affect reporting, refund processing or future communication.

Can I seek ITR filing guidance online outside Indore?

Yes. Online enquiries may be handled across India, subject to identity, document, scope and professional-acceptance checks.

When does work start?

Work starts after document review, fee/timeline confirmation and professional acceptance.
