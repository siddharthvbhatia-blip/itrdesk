Common questions

FAQ

Can I share my income-tax portal password?

Please avoid sharing passwords through Instagram DM, public comments or unsecured channels. Use only the process shared after professional acceptance.

Are fees shown on the website?

No. Fees depend on the income type, documents, complexity and urgency. A quote is shared privately after review.

Is refund guaranteed?

No. Refund depends on tax computation, TDS/TCS, advance tax, AIS/26AS records and processing by the tax department.

Will you file without my approval?

No. ITR filing should be done after document review, computation and client confirmation.
