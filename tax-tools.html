Old regime • Section 10(13A)

HRA exemption estimator

The exemption is the least of actual HRA received, rent paid minus 10% of salary, and 50% of salary for the four specified metros or 40% elsewhere.

Important: this exemption is not available under the new regime. “Salary” for this test has a specific statutory meaning.

FY 2025-26 • Sections 208/211

Advance-tax instalment planner

Enter estimated tax remaining after TDS, TCS and eligible relief or credit. The tool applies the ₹10,000 threshold and cumulative instalment percentages.

Not interest calculation: this planner does not calculate sections 234B or 234C interest, nor special relief where income arises unexpectedly.
Official sources: Due dates ↗ · Senior-citizen exception ↗

Section 234F only

Late-filing fee checker

Compare a chosen filing date with the due date applicable to your case. The statutory cap is lower where total income does not exceed ₹5 lakh.

Limited scope: this does not calculate section 234A interest, loss-carry-forward consequences, belated-return eligibility, updated-return additional tax or other exposure.
