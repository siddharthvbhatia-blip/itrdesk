Service terms

Terms for paid digital drafts and professional enquiries.

Effective 16 July 2026.

Computation scope

The amount shown only after opening the computation-download option is ₹1,000 for generation of one PDF and one editable Word computation for the same AY 2026-27 figures entered by the customer. The verified download is normally available for 24 hours.

ITR preparation JSON scope

The separate ₹1,000 JSON payment unlocks one structured AY 2026-27 preparation draft for the exact data submitted. It records the taxpayer profile, broad income and tax summaries, record checklist, review flags and any linked calculator snapshot. It does not include professional review or filing.

Not an official filing JSON

The ITR Desk JSON is not an Income Tax Department pre-filled JSON, official ITR schema output, upload-ready return, acknowledgement or proof of filing. The current official utility and notified return schema must be used after complete reconciliation and review. Users should not file solely from the automated preparation draft.

No portal credentials

ITR Desk does not ask for an income-tax password, Aadhaar OTP, bank OTP or e-verification code. Users must access AIS, TIS, Form 26AS and official pre-filled data by logging in themselves only on the official portal. Locally selected source-file contents are not uploaded by the preparation page; only filenames and checklist selections may be included in the draft.

Not included

Neither digital-product payment includes verification of source documents, preparation or filing of an income-tax return, final ITR-form selection, tax audit, certification, notice response, representation, legal opinion or any other professional service.

Customer responsibility

The customer must provide complete and accurate particulars and classify income correctly. Special-rate income, capital-gain cost and exemptions, loss set-off, deductions, residential status, DTAA eligibility, taxes paid and other inputs must be supported by records.

Payment and access

Razorpay displays the applicable amount before authorisation. A document is released only after the server verifies the signature, order, fixed amount, currency, captured status, product and exact payload hash. A payment for one product does not unlock the other product or materially changed data.

Professional enquiry

An enquiry or callback request does not create a professional engagement. Work begins only after document review, conflict and eligibility checks, scope and fee confirmation, professional acceptance and receipt of required information.

Prohibited use

Do not submit another person's PAN or tax information without authority, enter portal credentials, attempt to bypass payment verification, interfere with the website, or represent a generated output as an official return, certificate, acknowledgement or filing confirmation.
