Service terms

Terms for secure computations and professional enquiries.

Effective 16 July 2026.

Computation scope

The amount shown in secure checkout is for generation of one PDF and one editable Word computation for the same AY 2026-27 figures entered by the customer. The verified download is normally available for 24 hours.

Not included

The computation payment does not include verification of source documents, preparation or filing of an income-tax return, selection of the legally applicable ITR form, tax audit, certification, notice response, representation, legal opinion or any other professional service.

Customer responsibility

The customer must provide complete and accurate particulars and classify income correctly. Special-rate income, capital-gain cost and exemptions, loss set-off, deductions, residential status, DTAA eligibility, taxes paid and other inputs must be supported by records.

Nature of output

The generated document is an indicative computation based on customer-entered figures. It is not an ITR acknowledgement, filing confirmation, audit report, certificate or guarantee of tax outcome. It must be reconciled with the applicable Income Tax Department utility before reliance or filing.

Payment and access

Razorpay displays the applicable amount before payment authorisation. Documents are released only after the payment gateway confirms and the server verifies a successful payment. A payment for one computation does not unlock a materially changed computation. Do not alter the computation during checkout.

Professional enquiry

An enquiry does not create a professional engagement. Work begins only after document review, conflict and eligibility checks, scope and fee confirmation, professional acceptance and receipt of required information.

Prohibited use

Do not submit another person's PAN or tax information without authority, attempt to bypass payment verification, interfere with the website, or use the generated document as a false certificate or filing acknowledgement.
