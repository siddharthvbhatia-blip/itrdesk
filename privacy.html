Privacy & Safety

Privacy policy and communication safety.

This page explains how enquiry details and documents are handled for ITR filing/review communication.

Information collected

We may collect name, mobile number, email, city, PAN, address, income category, tax-computation figures, enquiry details and documents that you voluntarily provide for a paid computation, ITR filing or professional review.

Calculator privacy

The free calculator processes figures in your browser. Figures are sent to the secure document service only when you choose the paid PDF/Word computation and proceed to payment.

Paid computation and payment

For a paid computation, the supplied particulars and computed figures are processed to verify the payment and generate the requested files. Payment credentials and banking/card/UPI details are handled by the authorised payment gateway and are not collected by ITR Desk. The payment order retains a cryptographic report reference rather than the report itself. A time-limited authorisation may be kept in your browser session for repeat download.

Purpose

Information is used for generating the requested computation, payment verification, tax review, ITR filing assistance, communication, professional records, fraud prevention and compliance-related purposes.

Data minimisation and safety

The paid computation form does not request Aadhaar or bank-account details. Do not share passwords, OTPs or income-tax portal credentials through public comments, social-media messages or unsecured channels. Sensitive documents should be shared only through the approved process after professional acceptance.

Third-party processing

Payments and technical hosting may involve the payment gateway and hosting provider under their respective privacy and security terms. Only information necessary for payment verification and service delivery should be processed.

No guarantee

No guarantee is made regarding refund, tax saving, processing time or absence of notice. Professional assistance is subject to document review and applicable law.
