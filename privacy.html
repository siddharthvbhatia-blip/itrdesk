Privacy by clear choice

What stays on your device—and what is sent.

Effective 16 July 2026. This page separates the free tools, secure computation, payment and optional professional-enquiry flows.

Free calculators and form finder

The comprehensive calculator, HRA tool, advance-tax planner, late-fee checker and ITR form finder calculate in your browser. Their entries are not transmitted to ITR Desk merely because you type or calculate. They do not require a name, PAN, Aadhaar or login.

Private page feedback

The “Was this useful?” choice is stored in your browser using local storage. It is not displayed publicly as a review or testimonial. The website contains measurement hooks for button interactions, but no third-party analytics tracker is currently loaded by these pages. This policy must be updated before a new analytics service is enabled.

WhatsApp enquiry

The optional enquiry form prepares a message on your device. The entries leave the website only when you choose to open and send that message through WhatsApp. WhatsApp then processes it under its own terms. The enquiry form asks only for basic case facts; do not include PAN, Aadhaar, account numbers, passwords or OTPs.

Secure PDF and Word computation

The separate computation flow is closed until you open it. If you choose to continue, the identity particulars and calculator inputs required for the document are sent to the ITR Desk payment backend. The backend validates and independently recomputes the report, creates a cryptographic report hash and requests a Razorpay order. The current service has no report database; document payloads are processed for the request, while hosting and payment providers may maintain operational or transaction logs under their own policies.

Payment information

Razorpay displays the amount and handles card, UPI, bank and other payment credentials. ITR Desk does not receive your card PIN, UPI PIN or banking password. After payment, the server checks the Razorpay signature, order, amount, currency, report reference and captured status before issuing a time-limited download authorisation.

Document download

The verified PDF or Word document is generated on request and returned with private, no-store cache instructions. A signed authorisation and the exact report may remain in the current browser session for up to 24 hours so that the same computation can be downloaded again. Clearing browser session data can remove that local access.

Professional work and records

If a user independently proceeds to a professional engagement, information may be processed for identity checks, conflict and eligibility review, tax work, communication, billing, fraud prevention, legal compliance and professional-record obligations. The approved document-sharing process and engagement scope are confirmed separately.

Your safety choices

  • Never send an OTP, card PIN, UPI PIN or banking password.
  • Do not send income-tax portal passwords through WhatsApp or public comments.
  • Share sensitive records only after confirming the recipient and process.
  • Use only information you are authorised to provide.

Contact and correction requests

For a privacy question or correction request, email siddharth.v.bhatia@gmail.com or contact +91 7879857126. Include enough information to identify the request, but do not send passwords or OTPs.

Data-flow summary

  1. Free tools: on-device.
  2. Enquiry: sent only if you choose WhatsApp.
  3. Computation: sent only after opening the separate flow.
  4. Payment credentials: handled by Razorpay.
  5. Paid document: server-verified, time-limited access.
