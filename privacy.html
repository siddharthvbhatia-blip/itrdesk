Privacy by clear choice

What stays on your device—and what is sent.

Effective 21 July 2026. This page separates free tools, website enquiries, notifications, Google Drive storage, paid downloads and optional professional work.

Free calculators and form finder

The tax calculator, focused tools and ITR form finder calculate in your browser. Typing or calculating does not transmit those figures to ITR Desk. These free tools do not require a name, PAN, Aadhaar or portal login.

Local source-document extraction

Supported searchable Form 16, AIS, TIS and Form 26AS records are read by browser code on your device. Raw file bytes, full extracted text and any PDF opening password are not uploaded during this free extraction step. Only values you deliberately confirm can be applied to a calculator or review draft.

Professional enquiry

When you press “Send enquiry”, the name, mobile number, broad case type, optional message, consent and source page are sent over HTTPS to the private ITR Desk backend. The form rejects obvious PAN, password, OTP and PIN content. A successful response gives the visitor a reference number and the enquiry becomes available in the password-protected administrator inbox. Submission does not create a professional engagement.

Mobile notification channel

To notify the administrator immediately, the backend publishes the validated basic enquiry to a private, high-entropy notification topic. The notification service temporarily caches messages for missed-device delivery; its standard public-service retention is limited and is not used as the permanent professional record. The topic address is revealed only after administrator-password verification.

Google Drive archive

When the optional Google Apps Script connection is activated, the backend also sends the validated enquiry to a private Google Sheet owned by CA Siddharth Bhatia. Google Apps Script appends the record and sends an email notification to the configured Gmail address. The Sheet and email remain within the authorised Google account and are not exposed to website visitors.

Private administrator inbox

The administrator inbox is marked noindex and requires a private password checked by the backend. The password is kept only in session storage for the current browser tab and is removed when the inbox is locked or the session ends. The public website does not contain the password, password hash, notification topic or Google Apps Script URL.

Fallback to WhatsApp

If every automatic delivery channel is temporarily unavailable, the website prepares a WhatsApp message containing the same basic details. The enquiry is completed only when the visitor taps Send in WhatsApp. Under normal operation, a successful website submission does not require WhatsApp.

Private page preference

The “Was this page useful?” choice is saved only in your browser. It is not transmitted to ITR Desk and is not displayed as a testimonial.

Paid computation and ITR review draft

Identity and tax information is transmitted only after you deliberately open and submit the separate paid flow. The backend validates and independently recomputes or validates the submitted data, creates the payment order and verifies payment before releasing a download.

Payment information

Razorpay handles card, UPI, bank and other payment credentials. ITR Desk does not receive your card PIN, UPI PIN or banking password. The server validates the payment signature, order, amount, currency and paid product before releasing a download.

Service providers

GitHub Pages hosts the public website. Vercel hosts the private backend. Razorpay handles payment processing. Google services handle the optional Drive archive and Gmail notification after authorisation. A private notification service is used for immediate administrator alerts. WhatsApp processes an enquiry only when all automatic channels fail or when a visitor deliberately selects WhatsApp.

Professional work and records

If a professional engagement is separately accepted, information may be processed for identity and conflict checks, tax work, communication, billing, fraud prevention, legal compliance and professional-record obligations. Scope, document-sharing method and retention are confirmed separately.

Your safety choices

  • Enter income-tax credentials only on the official incometax.gov.in portal.
  • Never send an OTP, card PIN, UPI PIN or banking password.
  • Do not put PAN, Aadhaar or account numbers in the initial enquiry note.
  • Use “Clear records” after local extraction on a shared device.
  • Review any prepared WhatsApp message before tapping Send.

Contact and correction requests

For a privacy question or correction request, email siddharth.v.bhatia@gmail.com or call +91 7879857126. Do not include passwords or OTPs.

Data-flow summary

  1. Free tools: on-device.
  2. Local file contents: not uploaded.
  3. Enquiry: sent only after Submit.
  4. Private notification: immediate administrator alert.
  5. Google Drive: permanent archive after one-time activation.
  6. Admin inbox: password-protected and noindex.
  7. WhatsApp: only if automatic delivery fails or is selected deliberately.
  8. Paid data: separate verified checkout flow.
