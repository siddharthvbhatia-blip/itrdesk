Build the return from records, not only net profit

Freelancers, consultants, professionals and small-business owners may need to reconcile receipts, invoices, bank accounts, TDS, expenses, assets, liabilities and tax payments before the income computation is reliable.

Information commonly reviewed

  • Nature of activity and gross receipts
  • Income, expense and bank summaries
  • Form 16A, AIS and Form 26AS
  • GST turnover or other reconciliations where relevant
  • Fixed assets, loans and capital details where applicable
  • Presumptive taxation, books and audit considerations

Presumptive taxation is not an automatic choice

Eligibility and consequences depend on the activity, receipts, taxpayer facts and applicable provisions. It should be compared with the available records and the intended declarations before the return form is selected.

Professional note: Books, audit, presumptive-tax and regime-selection conclusions require the complete facts. Filing begins only after scope and professional acceptance are confirmed.
