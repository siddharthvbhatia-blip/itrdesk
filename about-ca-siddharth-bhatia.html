Purpose of ITR Desk

ITR Desk provides general income-tax awareness resources, an AY 2026-27 calculator, document checklists and a direct enquiry route for taxpayers. It covers common starting points involving salary, house property, capital gains, futures and options, intraday trading, business or professional income, NRI matters and AIS/Form 26AS mismatches.

The website does not replace examination of the taxpayer’s complete records. Tax classification, return-form selection, regime choice, exemptions, loss set-off and filing positions depend on the applicable law and the taxpayer’s facts.

How professional work is accepted

  1. The taxpayer shares basic case information and requests the relevant checklist.
  2. Documents, identity, scope and complexity are reviewed through the approved process.
  3. Timeline, professional fee and engagement scope are communicated privately.
  4. Work begins only after professional acceptance and receipt of the required records.
  5. The computation and filing particulars are taken up with the client for confirmation before submission.

Editorial and safety approach

Website material is written as factual tax awareness. It avoids refund guarantees, promised tax savings, comparative claims and claims that a notice can never arise. Visitors should not share passwords, OTPs or sensitive documents through public comments or social-media messages.

Professional boundary: Accessing a guide, using the calculator or sending an enquiry does not itself create a professional engagement. Engagement begins only after review and explicit acceptance.
