Important scope

This calculator covers an individual’s normal slab-rate income. It does not compute special-rate tax on capital gains, lottery, online gaming, virtual digital assets, unexplained income, agricultural-income rate integration, AMT, relief under sections 89/90/91, or interest and late fee.

Business or professional taxpayers should also verify regime-selection and switching rules before acting.

Why the final tax may differ

Actual tax depends on classification of income, eligibility of deductions, set-off rules, special tax rates, residential status and information available in AIS, Form 26AS and other records.

01

Capital gains

Listed shares, mutual funds, property and other assets may be taxed at special rates and require separate calculations.

02

Regime eligibility

Taxpayers with business or professional income have additional conditions and switching restrictions.

03

Deductions and losses

Limits, evidence and set-off restrictions must be checked before a deduction or loss is used.
